Why vote for us?

Support our social cause

Great news! We managed to get a partnership with UN women and also won their "Forum Generation Equality". This will help us educate bachelors on gender inequality and related issues, especially domestic violence, which became an even bigger problem this year due to COVID-19

Meet different faces

Our team is composed of 9 people of 9 different nationalities. This ensures a strong representation of every student in the bachelor.

Balance between academics and social life

We want to make you feel as comfortable as possible in your daily life. This means a smoother academic journey, as well as an enhanced social balance.

Install new traditions and make the Bachelor feel like home

We want to create new traditions, such as, for example, a yearly color run. Moreover, we will facilitate the creation of new commitees.

Improving everyday life

We have a very strong CS team and are backed by one of the biggest bachelor projects so far: BX+. We believe that this partnership will make student life easier, especially for new generations of bachelors, but also future l'Ores, who will be able to borrow our technologies

"Tradition immersing" weeks

People will get a chance to present their culture to the bachelor during a given week. That includes traditional food and interesting events.
